Hwang's Research is an independent research organization focused on building reliable and intelligent systems.

We explore various research areas including:

* Artificial Intelligence
* Autonomous Driving
* AI Agents
* Quantum Computing
* Computer Systems
* Cyber Security
* Multimodal AI
* Edge AI & Federated Learning

## Vision

Our goal is to develop next-generation intelligent systems that are:

* Reliable
* Explainable
* Efficient
* Scalable

We focus on practical research, open-source development, and real-world applications.
